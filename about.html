About Us

Welcome to Bayanihan! We are dedicated to promoting local events, celebrations, and activities that bring our residents together and foster a sense of belonging.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and accessible resource for all things related to barangay events. Whether it's feeding programs, community clean-up, sports tournaments, or cultural showcases, we strive to keep you informed and engaged.

Together, we can celebrate our heritage, support local initiatives, and create lasting memories.
